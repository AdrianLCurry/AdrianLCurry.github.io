Following My Journey

In June of 2022, I decided to focus my time on learning the skills necessary to take my career to the next level. With a Bachelor's in Kinesiology, 8+ years of general and managerial knowledge, and recent experience in accounts payable, my mission was to find a career path in which I could focus the skills of my past experiences. Already skilled in Excel and basic front-end web development, spending the next month researching the job market and networking on LinkedIn landed me at the doorstep of Data Analytics.


In August of 2022, I completed the "Becoming a Data Analyst" certificate course from the LinkedIn Learning platform. Soon after, I began to build a porfolio to showcase my ever-growing skillset. Navigate to the Projects tab to find completed SQL queries for cleaning and exploration, Tableau and Power BI dashboards designed for clear visualization and storytelling, as well as dashboards in Excel. Along the way, I have met some incredible individuals and compiled a list of useful resources - all of which I will continue to share on my Resources page, LinkedIn profile, and TikTok content.
